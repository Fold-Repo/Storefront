# Project Shorp: Future Enhancement & Scaling Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic vision and technical roadmap for evolving the Shorp Epos platform from a dynamic store generator into a comprehensive, AI-integrated e-commerce ecosystem.

## üöÄ Strategic Vision
To empower entrepreneurs by providing a "zero-effort" storefront generation experience that handles the complexities of sales, logistics, and customer engagement through automation and intelligent design.

---

## üõ† Feature & Enhancement Plan

### 1. Unified Customer Journey (Storefront Excellence)
*   **AI Personalized Discovery**: Implement a recommendation engine that suggests products based on browsing history and real-time trends.
*   **One-Click Optimized Checkout**: Streamline the checkout process with Apple Pay, Google Pay, and localized payment gateway auto-detection.
*   **Interactive Product Experience**: Support for 3D GLB/USDZ models and AR "View in Room" for high-ticket items.
*   **Dynamic Search & Filtering**: Lightning-fast search with fuzzy matching and smart filters based on product attributes.

### 2. Intelligent Store Management (Dashboard 2.0)
*   **Bulk Inventory Operations**: Upload products via CSV/Excel and bulk update prices or stock alert levels.
*   **AI Content Generation**: Automatic product descriptions, SEO tags, and social media captions based on images.
*   **Multi-Store Management**: Permit a single merchant to manage multiple niche storefronts from a single dashboard.
*   **Real-time Analytics Dashboard**: In-depth sales heatmaps, abandonment rates, and customer lifetime value (CLV) metrics.

### 3. Integrated Logistics & Operations
*   **Automated Fulfillment**: Integration with global (FedEx, DHL) and local logistics partners for automatic shipping label generation.
*   **Real-time Tracking**: Customer-facing tracking portals with automated SMS/WhatsApp updates on delivery status.
*   **Warehouse Management (WMS)**: Track stock across multiple physical locations or dark stores.

### 4. Dynamic Store Generation (Core Platform)
*   **Niche-Specific AI Templates**: Auto-suggest layouts and color palettes based on the brand's industry (e.g., Fashion vs. Hardware).
*   **Interactive Editor Enhancements**: Drag-and-drop landing page builder with A/B testing blocks.
*   **Localized Content**: Automatic currency conversion and multi-language support based on visitor IP.
